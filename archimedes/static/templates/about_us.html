Global GreenLife Inc. is a high-tech company located in the Silicon Valley. Founded in 2011 by a group of Ph.D. engineers and researchers, Global GreenLife Inc. develops and sells building energy analysis, HVAC, environmental control and energy management software. Our software includes desktop; cloud-based and mobile software for energy and economic analyses; environmental control and energy management; HVAC load calculations; duct sizing; psychrometrics; equipment sizing and selection, and much more. Our clients include customers throughout the world, from the Fortune 500 companies, to smaller architectural/engineering firms. In 2013 we expanded our business to Asia with an office in Shanghai, China (Shanghai EverGreen IT Co. Ltd.).
