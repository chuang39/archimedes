Archimedes Controls Corp. provides you the technology you need to control, monitor, and secure your data center. Based in Silicon Valley, Archimedes Controls was found by a group of entrepreneurs, seasoned executives and industrial experts. For over 20 years, our founders have successfully delivered numerous innovative software and systems to help customers achieve the greatest profit and operational excellence.

Currently, Archimedes Controls Corp. is aimed at delivering next generation unified solutions to meet the demands for securer environment, reduced maintenance cost and energy efficienteffiicent operations for data center operators. As part of the data center technology evolution, these criteria are becoming more critical than ever.

Archimedes Controls is committed to bring the latest technology and solutions to data center operators and their customers. Please continue to visit our website for updates of our progress.



We deployed A150 system in one of our large customer’s IDC projects. The system provides a three-dimensional model displaying facility's temperature and airflow profile in real time. It also provides the capability of dynamically adjusting the controlling mechanism based on operation's scenario. A150 makes our data centers more intelligent and our operations more efficient. With A150’s advanced technology coupled with its outstanding real time control capability, we realized continuous high PUE optimization and improved PUE from 1.66 to 1.50, and as the result, it saved us tremendous amount of power expenses. In order to drive and realize the IDC technology evolution, we plan to widely adapt and deploy A150 system in our IDC facility nationwide.
